About

OMAHACK is hackathon series in Omaha, NE. We believe small teams can out-innovate large established organizations. We also believe talented people miss the opportunity to bump shoulders with others who compliment their skillsets and enable powerful teams and relationships. OMAHACK takes place twice a year -- Winter & Summer. It is a multi-day hackathon event ending with a demo day and pitch to the community.
