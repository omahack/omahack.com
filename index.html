About

OMAHACK is hackathon series in Omaha, NE. We believe small teams can out-innovate large established organizations. We also believe talented people miss the opportunity to bump shoulders with others who compliment their skillsets and enable powerful teams and relationships. OMAHACK is taking place in January 2023. It is a multi-day hackathon event ending with a demo day and pitch to the community. The winning team gets the $1000 prize, with additional prizes to be announced.
